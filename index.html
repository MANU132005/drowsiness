The Problem We Solve

Drowsiness while driving or performing critical tasks is a significant cause of accidents, injuries, and loss of productivity. Individuals, especially those in long-distance driving or those working extended shifts, often face fatigue that impairs their alertness and reaction time.

Traditional solutions for drowsiness detection, such as specialized headgear or vehicle-integrated systems, tend to be expensive and not easily accessible to the general public.

Our Solution

To address this problem, Drowsi-Tech Vision presents a real-time drowsiness detection application for Android devices. The app utilizes the front camera of a smartphone to monitor the user's eyes and face for signs of drowsiness. It is developed using Java in Android Studio and uses computer vision techniques to detect eye closure or fatigue. When the app detects prolonged eye closure, it triggers an alarm sound to alert the user immediately.

This app is designed to be lightweight, efficient, and capable of running offline, making it an ideal solution for drivers, machine operators, and workers who need fatigue monitoring in real-time.
